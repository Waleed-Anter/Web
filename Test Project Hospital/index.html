Emergency

Medicine is just a reason, and the doctor is medicine

The emergency department or the accident and emergency department (ER), is a medical treatment department in hospitals for quick cases that cannot wait for any role, where the emergency doctor is called and he in turn performs the necessary first aid, such as stopping the bleeding if the patient is bleeding, then determines the specialist doctor who is called to remedy the situation. Most of the cases that come to the emergency department are from traffic accidents or cases coming by ambulance.

Ambulance

Take care not to deteriorate the patient's condition

First aid follows several principles, which are:

* Complete control of the event site.

* Not to consider the injured person dead, even if the manifestations of life have disappeared from him, such as pulse or breathing.

* Removing the injured person from the source of danger.

* Attention to the process of cardiopulmonary resuscitation, the process of artificial respiration, and shock.
