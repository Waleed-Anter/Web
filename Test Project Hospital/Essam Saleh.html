Brief

Dr.Essam Saleh, consultant obstetrics and gynecology and gynecological endoscopy. (8 years experience)


Scientific studies

Dr. Essam Saleh Salem received his Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery (MBBCH) with honors from Cairo University, Cairo, Egypt in 1980 followed by his Master s degree in Obstetrics and Gynecology from Ain Shams University, Cairo, Egypt in 1986 followed by his Ph.D in medicine and surgery. From Ain Shams University in 2001. Dr. Essam Saleh Salem also obtained a diploma in advanced laparoscopic gynecology and obstetrics from Kiel University, Germany in 2004, in addition to obtaining training in obstetrics and gynecology and laparoscopy, USA in 1998.

Practical experiences

Dr. Essam Saleh Salem worked as a consultant in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Military Hospitals, Cairo, Egypt from 1982 to 2009 and then moved to Saudi Arabia and worked as a consultant in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Yanbu National Hospital, Saudi Arabia from 2009 to 2012 as a consultant In the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Al-Ahmadi Hospital, Madinah, Saudi Arabia from 2012 to 2015. Dr. Essam Saleh Salem also worked as a consultant in the same department at Al-Mousa Hospital from 2015 to 2016 and then as a consultant in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at the Saudi German Hospital in Jeddah From 2016 to 2020 as a Consultant in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at GNP from 2020 to 2022.
